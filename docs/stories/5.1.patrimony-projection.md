## Story

**As a** user,
**I want** to see projections of my patrimony over 3, 6, and 12 months,
**so that** I can plan ahead and understand my financial trajectory under different scenarios.

## Acceptance Criteria

1. Page `/projections` displays projected patrimony for 3, 6, and 12 month horizons
2. Three scenario modes: optimistic (+10% savings), realistic (current trend), pessimistic (-10% savings)
3. Projection engine uses last 6 months of transaction history to calculate average income, expenses, and savings rate
4. Line chart (Recharts) showing projected balance over time with all three scenarios overlaid
5. Summary cards: projected balance at each horizon, estimated monthly savings, current savings rate
6. Scenario selector (tabs or toggle) to highlight one scenario while dimming others
7. Empty state when insufficient transaction history (< 2 months of data)
8. Loading skeleton while projection data is calculated
9. Responsive layout: chart full-width on mobile, side-by-side with cards on desktop
